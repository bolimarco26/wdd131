La Paz - Bolivia

La Paz, Bolivia, is the country’s administrative capital, dramatically nestled in a valley surrounded by the towering Andes at an altitude of about 3,650 meters above sea level. Known for its breathtaking landscapes, vibrant markets, and rich blend of modern life with ancient traditions, the city offers a unique cultural experience. Its winding streets, colorful neighborhoods, and views of Mount Illimani make La Paz a destination full of contrasts, history, and beauty.
